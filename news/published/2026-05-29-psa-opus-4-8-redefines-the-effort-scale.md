Opus 4.8 introduces a redefinition of the effort scale, where 'low' effort now consumes as many output tokens as 'medium-high' effort did in previous versions, and 'medium' effort consumes even more. This change impacts speed and token costs, so users should adjust their settings accordingly to optimize performance and cost.
