This post advises users to review and clean up their global and project CLAUDE.md configuration files after upgrading to Opus 4.8, as the new version follows these instructions more strictly and may cause unexpected behaviors if old or irrelevant settings remain. It provides a practical reminder to maintain configuration hygiene to avoid issues in long sessions or legacy projects.
